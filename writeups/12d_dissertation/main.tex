Type IIB string theory possesses a conjectured non-perturbative $SL(2,\mathbb{Z})$ duality whose ten-dimensional origin remains unexplained. This thesis examines the proposal that the duality, and the theory itself, descend from a twelve-dimensional structure. We find that the $12$d interpretation is robust for the axio-dilaton and form-field sectors, but remains effective rather than fundamental.
\end{abstract}
