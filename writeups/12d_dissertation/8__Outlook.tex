\section{Concluding Remarks and Outlook}

We set out to ask whether the type IIB string theory admits a 12d origin, and what such an origin would reveal about its SL(2, Z) duality. 
We now summarize what the preceding chapters establish, measured against the objectives stated in the introduction, before turning to open directions.

\subsection{Aims and what was achieved}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item The 12d interpretation of the axio-dilaton sector (Section \ref{sec:axio-dilaton-12d}) is achieved. The D7 and D(-1) backgrounds arise as a 12d Kaluza-Klein monopole and pp-wave reduced on a torus, the type IIB SL(2, Z) acts as large gauge transformations in 12d, and the reduction reproduces the type IIB Killing spinor equations. This sector admits a robust 12d reading.
    \item The extension beyond the axio-dilaton sector (Section \ref{sec:covariant-unification}) is partial. The RR and NSNS form fields can be assembled into 12d objects, with the 10d self-duality of the five-form realized as a 12d Hodge duality. This is, however, a covariant repackaging of the 10d theory on a two-torus rather than a reduction of a genuine 12d theory: not all 12d degrees of freedom are excited, and the mismatch between the SO(8) and SO(10) little-group content shows that a full 12d uplift of the field content is unavailable.
    \item The role of the D3 brane (Section \ref{sec:d3-self-duality}) is clarified. The worldvolume electromagnetic SL(2, Z) duality of the D3 brane is consistent only when accompanied by the bulk SL(2, Z) transformation, tying the worldvolume and bulk dualities together.
    \item Consistency with higher-derivative corrections (Section \ref{sec:effective-actions}) is only partial, and ultimately obstructed. At four points the 8-derivative effective action can be written in 12d-covariant form; at five points there is a U(1)-preserving axio-dilaton term that cannot.
\end{enumerate}

Taken together, the 12d interpretation is robust for the axio-dilaton and form-field sectors, but remains effective rather than fundamental. 
This is consistent with the long-standing obstruction to a 10+2 supergravity: no consistent theory with 32 supercharges reducing to type IIB is known, owing to the non-compact little group and the absence of a momentum generator \cite{Hewson:1997wv}. 
What we arrive at is a 12d-covariant organization of the 10d theory, not a dynamical 12d spacetime.

\subsection{Outlook}

Several directions follow. A first concerns the effective action: whether a more general 12d ansatz, going beyond the $t_8 t_8 \widehat{R}^4$ contraction, can accommodate the type IIB couplings, and whether the five-point obstruction persists at higher points. It is possible that beginning at six points further obstructive terms appear which, together with the five-point terms of \cite{Liu:2025uqu}, conspire to restore a 12d-covariant form; settling this requires the axio-dilaton effective action up to eight points. The alternative is that the obstruction is genuine, and the type IIB effective action cannot be written 12d-covariantly.

A second direction is whether the obstruction to a 10+2 supergravity is fundamental or an artifact of demanding a Lorentzian theory that realizes the full 12d spacetime symmetries. The constructions in this work only ever used a 12d-covariant repackaging on a fixed torus, never the full 12d little group, which leaves room for formulations in which the non-compact little group and the missing momentum generator do not arise.

Finally, the role of SL(2, Z) acquires further significance in the context of the AdS/CFT correspondence. 
Recently, it was shown that an M2 brane wrapping a circle at the boundary of the $AdS_4 \times S^7$ background reproduces, via a one-loop computation of its worldvolume effective action, the subleading $1/N$ corrections to Wilson loop observables in the dual ABJM theory \cite{Giombi:2023vzu, Tseytlin:2024euk}. 
This raises the question of whether an analogous construction exists for the $AdS_5 \times S^5$ background, whose dual $\mathcal{N}=4$ SYM theory carries its own SL(2, Z) duality. 
Addressing it would require identifying the type IIB counterparts of the $AdS_4 \times S^7$ geometry and the M2 brane, and in particular a 12d interpretation of the D3 brane that sources $AdS_5 \times S^5$. A sharper understanding of the 12d structure of type IIB may therefore bear on whether such a correspondence exists.
